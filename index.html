Inside The Build

PrimeNest Realty AI — Inside The Build

A step-by-step look at the Botpress flow builder canvas powering the PrimeNest real estate lead qualification bot.

Main Conversation Flow

The entry point of the bot — greeting node, intent detection, and initial routing logic that determines whether the user wants to buy, rent, or inquire about a listing.

↑ Instant response to every incoming inquiry

Lead Qualification Branch

The qualification nodes that capture budget range, property type preference, and purchase timeline — scoring each lead before routing to an agent.

↑ Only sales-ready leads reach the agent

Property Inquiry Flow

Nodes handling specific property questions — location, bedroom count, amenities — with dynamic responses based on the user's stated preferences.

↑ Personalised replies without human input

Appointment Booking Branch

The scheduling flow — prompts the user to book a viewing, collects contact details, and triggers a Calendly link or direct agent notification automatically.

↑ Viewings booked without agent involvement

CRM Handoff Flow

The final stage — qualified lead data is packaged and sent to the CRM via webhook, with an instant WhatsApp or email alert fired to the assigned agent.

↑ Zero manual data entry, 100% lead capture

Case Study

Real Estate Lead
Qualification Bot

A deep-dive into how I built a WhatsApp AI system that tripled qualified lead volume for a Lagos property agency.

01

The Problem

The client's sales team was spending 3+ hours per day manually filtering WhatsApp messages from potential buyers. Most contacts were casual browsers, not serious buyers — but the team had no way to filter them without a manual conversation. Leads that came in after hours received no response until the next morning, by which time they had often moved to a competitor.

📊

[ Screenshot: WhatsApp inbox overload — placeholder ]

02

The Solution

I built a Botpress AI chatbot connected to WhatsApp Business API. The bot greets every incoming message instantly, qualifies intent (buy/rent/invest), captures location preference and budget, then routes only high-intent leads to the sales team's CRM with a full conversation summary and contact details.

03

Workflow Architecture

WhatsApp message → Botpress NLP → Intent classification → Qualification flow (location / budget / timeline) → Lead scoring → If qualified: push to Airtable + notify agent via WhatsApp. If unqualified: nurture sequence.
